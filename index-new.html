The Experience


Sphera’s offering is as varied as it is rich, as intriguing as it is powerful. Film, arts, gaming, events or concerts are all delivered with the sharpest picture, with the deepest bass, in perfect comfort. Lovingly designed, our spaces transport audiences to a ringside seat, suspend reality, expand horizons or become a place to get together for talks and events. Technical
Specifications

Projection


We provide the highest picture quality available today with deeper, richer colours and better contrast.


Our offering includes EclairColor, a unique colour treatment that reveals finer details and gives a better sense of depth. Almost like 3D but without the glasses. We use 4K projectors and our screens are wall-to -wall for greater impact. Actual 3D is particularly powerful with our set up and our rooms are HDR ready; the next big step forward in digital cinema. Everything combines to produce a truly immersive effect and what’s more, programming can be changed at a moment’s notice.

Audio


Our audio system is unrivalled, enveloping you with sound that puts you centre stage, with intelligent and multi-dimensional delivery. It features a higher frequency than standard systems with a more powerful bass for sound you can really feel.


Using technology unique to us in the cinema sector, this subwoofer is based on the latest knowledge of energy transducers. There’s no need for audio technicians and our system is truly multi-purpose. Switch easily between film, arts, gaming, events or concerts using just a smart phone.

Design


Our designers have created a distinct interior concept, classically inspired, but perfectly calibrated for faultless contemporary viewing.


Our wide, comfortable seats, developed exclusively for us, are made from material designed to minimise reflection. Programmable artistic lighting, interactive signage and a lobby area with an LED curtain all help create an exciting, unique atmosphere. We can design and fit a bar in the auditorium for a more social feel.
